DNA Connect: Unlocking the Code

In this game, you'll embark on a journey deep inside the microscopic world of cells, where you'll encounter the DNA letters—A, T, G, and C—and their RNA counterparts—A, U, C, and G. Your mission is to connect these letters correctly, unlocking the code that leads to the formation of vital polypeptides.
